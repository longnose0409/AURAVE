About AURAVE

Discover the story behind our luxury fashion brand and our commitment to elegance, quality, and sustainability.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by three passionate fashion enthusiasts - Gem, Val, and Arjaylen - AURAVE was born from a shared vision to create luxury fashion that speaks to every individual's unique aura.

What started as a small collection of carefully curated pieces has grown into a comprehensive fashion brand that celebrates diversity, elegance, and personal expression. We believe that fashion is more than just clothing - it's a way to express your inner self and make a statement about who you are.

Today, AURAVE continues to push boundaries in the fashion industry, combining timeless elegance with modern innovation to create pieces that are both beautiful and meaningful.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to express their unique aura through carefully crafted, sustainable fashion that combines luxury with accessibility. We strive to create pieces that make you feel confident, beautiful, and authentically yourself.

Our Vision

To become the leading luxury fashion brand that celebrates diversity and individuality while setting new standards for sustainability and ethical practices in the fashion industry.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Quality
